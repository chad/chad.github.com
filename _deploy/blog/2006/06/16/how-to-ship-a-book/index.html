How to Ship a Book

2006.06.16

Rails Recipes is
shipping!

It’s always satisfying the first time you get to hold a physical copy
of a book you’ve slaved over. But as a special treat, this time Pragmatic Dave has put together a slide
show of what it takes to actually ship a book.

Particulary impressive is the fact that Dave seems to have cloned himself
to have more hands on deck during the shipping process. I’ve always
wondered how he manages to accomplish so much given the same number of
hours available as the rest of us.
