On the Ruby on Rails Podcast

2005.11.29

Geoffrey Grosenbach recently hoodwink’d me into doing an
interview for the Ruby on Rails
Podcast. Geoffrey had a lot of great questions. Tune in
to hear me talk about how to become a remarkable software developer, why
Rails developers are currently at risk, why RubyGems has taken so long to
beincluded in the core Ruby distribution, and why you should always strive
to be the worst developer on every team you’re in.

Also, if you missed my radio appearance last week, you can now listen to it
here.

Fight the Traffic

2005.10.28

I got in a cab last night heading from Washington D.C. to the Dulles
airport. I was a block from the White House and traffic was stopped behind
a crowd that was pushing its way in to see the president.

The old Ethiopian cab driver suddenly kicked the taxi into gear and zipped
around a line of cars, edging us five cars closer to freedom.

"I hate traffic", he grumbled.

"You picked the wrong job, then, didn’t you?"

"No! I love my job. My job is to fight the traffic."
