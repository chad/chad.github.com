Announcing RailsConf 2006

Nov 27th, 2005

We at Ruby Central, Inc. are proud
to present the First International Rails
Conference.

The conference will be held June 22-25, 2006 in Chicago. We’ll have
keynotes including David Heinemeier
Hansson and Dave
Thomas. There will be additional keynotes announced later. You
won’t want to miss it. Head
on over and read the full announcement for the rest of the details we
have now. Be sure to subscribe to the RSS feed
so you don’t miss anything.

We’ve learned a lot over the past five years doing The International Ruby Conference, and
we’re expecting this conference to represent both the best of what
RubyConf has been as well as an opportunity to try some new and potentially
radical ideas. We’ve already got some ideas brewing, but we’d
also like to hear from
you!

(Thanks to Sam Stephenson for the beautiful
RailsConf web design!)
