Remaining Relevant

Oct 1st, 2005

While you wait for your copy of My Job
Went to India to arrive, you may want to warm up by reading Remaining Relevant,
an article I wrote for the May/June issue of Better Software Magazine. It
touches the same issues but from just above the 50,000 foot mark.

I recommend reading it Monday morning before you go to work. Download it
and save it for breakfast reading. A good way to start the week.

Thanks to Brian Marick for
reminding me that the article wasn’t linked yet.
