Next Generation Aggregators

Jul 2nd, 2003

I think there are a lot of people (including me) who have been thinking of
implementing something like this:

www.decafbad.com/blog/geek/syndicated_whuffie.phtml

I’m really looking forward to the day when this bubbles up to the top
of the right person’s priority list, and enough people start using it
to make it valuable.

I’m especially interested in the relevance (see LatentSemanticIndexing)
idea.

With the combination of these kinds of shifts and the proliferation of P2P
and wireless technologies, I’m betting (and hoping) that the way
I’ll be using the web by the end of 2004 will be very different than
the way I use it now.
