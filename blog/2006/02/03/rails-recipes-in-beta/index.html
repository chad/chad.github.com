Rails Recipes in Beta

Feb 3rd, 2006

Last night we released the first beta of my new book, Rails Recipes.
For those that don’t know, a Pragmatic Beta
Book lets you read the book as it’s being written.
I’m about a third of the way through, but there’s already a lot
of helpful content in the book, and with the beta process, the book will
grow in its owners’ hands as it’s developed.

If you’re looking for easy
to read walkthroughs of advanced Rails topics, this is the
book for you. If you’ve read Programming
Ruby and Agile Web
Development With Rails, this book is the logical next step.

I’m taking suggestions for Recipes, so if you get in early, your
recipe idea might just make it into the book.
